# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Training Athlete

Here is a one-day meal plan for a high-endurance marathon athlete requiring optimal fueling around training sessions:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3000 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (150g / 20%), Fat (67g / 20%), Carbs (450g / 60%)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM) - Pre-Training Fuel:**
Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with honey (30g), banana (120g), and whole milk (240ml). Scrambled eggs (2 large eggs, 100g).
  * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 85g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM) - During/Post-Training:**
Sports drink or banana smoothie with whey protein powder (30g), oat milk (250ml), and dates (40g).
  * Protein: 25g, Fat: 2g, Carbs: 60g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM) - Recovery Meal:**
Grilled chicken breast (150g) with brown rice (120g cooked), steamed sweet potato (150g), and olive oil drizzle (10ml). Mixed green salad (100g) with vinaigrette.
  * Protein: 42g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 110g

* **Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM) - Energy Boost:**
Whole wheat bread (60g) with peanut butter (25g) and strawberries (100g). Greek yogurt (150g).
  * Protein: 20g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 65g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM) - Evening Recovery:**
Lean ground turkey (160g) with whole wheat pasta (100g cooked), marinara sauce (150g), and parmesan cheese (20g). Roasted broccoli (200g) with garlic.
  * Protein: 38g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 95g

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM) - Sleep Support:**
Cottage cheese (200g, low-fat) with almonds (20g) and blueberries (80g).
  * Protein: 28g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 35g
